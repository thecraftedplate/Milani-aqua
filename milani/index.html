Questions & Answers

Everything You Need to Know

What is the minimum order quantity for bulk dispatch?
We accommodate orders starting from 5 cases for personal bottle sizes (300mL, 500mL) up to ongoing high-volume monthly orders for 20L dispenser tiers.
Do you offer custom branding for private corporate events?
Yes! We provide tailored co-branding services for luxury hotels, executive conferences, weddings, and corporate suites. Contact our team in the quote section below for details.
How quickly can orders be fulfilled?
Standard local deliveries are handled within 24–48 hours of confirmation. Scheduled weekly or monthly auto-fulfillments are also available for corporate accounts.
